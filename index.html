Welcome


Located in the heart of the Breckenridge, The E-Z Ski Resort cosy cabins in a rustic mountain setting. Whether you're planning a Colorado family ski vacation or a weekend group ski trip with friends, Mountain Thunder Lodge is the complete Colorado ski destination!


As one of Breckenridge's ski-in / gondola-out properties, visitors are still discovering this gem of luxury, convenience and personal service. Mountain Thunder Lodge is just two blocks to historic Main Street, easily accessed by foot. On Main Street, you will find shopping, restaurants, galleries, museums, ski/board/bike rentals, and many other spectacular services and unique shops.


The lodge is also a short walk to the BreckConnect Gondola and offers guest's convenient ski-in access via the Skyway Skiway. The Gondola takes you to Peaks 7 and 8, where you can catch the other lifts, sign up for lessons, enjoy the half pipe and terrain park and access all of Breck. When you are ready for a hot tub, ski back to Mountain Thunder Lodge and relax in one of hot tubs or the outdoor heated pool.


So join us today and Take comfort in the cold!

Directions from Denvor International Airport



1.Take I-80 east to I-215 south.

2.Take Exit 6 (6200 South) and go east on 6200 South, toward the mountains.

3.This road will lead you straight to UT-210 and up Little Cottonwood Canyon to Snowbird.

4. Take right on Snowbird and continue up the road until you reach The Low Key Tavern.

5.Take the next right and travel 2 miles up the mountain road to reach the E-Z ski Resort.
